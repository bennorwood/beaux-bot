Meet Beaux.

Who is Beaux?

Beaux Bot is Lafayette's very own community assistant.

What can he do?

Beaux Bot can do many things! He can help you make plans, find a parking spot, and even mow your lawn!*

*Just kidding, Beaux Bot cannot mow your lawn.

Where can I meet him?

Beaux Bot is easy to find! Just shoot him a message via text, facebook messenger, or slack!

Want to help?

Help make Beaux Bot even smarter by contributing to his github page!
